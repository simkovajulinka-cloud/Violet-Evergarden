Violet Evergarden

Violet Evergarden is the main protagonist of the Violet Evergarden series. At the start of the series she is aproximately 14, and at the end she's 18.
Violet is a former child soldier who after the end of war becomes a Automemory doll in her search for the meaning of words 'I love you'

Violet's life

Early childhood

Violet was found as a orphan on the battle field and later gifted to Major Gilbert (Gilbert Bougainvillea) as a gift by his older brother Dietfried Bougainvillea
A young girl who couldnt speak or understood any words besides the word "kill".
Despite the orders of treating her only as a war weapon Gilbert though her how to read, speak and gave her name 'Violet' hoping should grow
into a woman beautiful as the flower. Leidenschaftlich's army never registred her as a person thus she wasnt officially part of the Leidenschaftlich army despite that she worked under Gilbert on the front lines thanks to her incredible combat skills and never feeling any emotions thus not feeling any regret for her actions. During the final battle Gilbert gets fatally hurt and Violet losts both of her arms, despite that she still tries to drag him with her mouth. Gilbert proccesed to stop her to tell her to live freely and happily not as a tool but as a regular person. Before he succumbed to his injuries he confested to her that he loves her from the bottom of his heart.

After the end of the war

Violet spends in the hospital 120 days recovering from her injuries, getting a metal prosthetic arms. After 120 days comes Colonel Hogins (Claudia Hodgins)to take her to the Evergardens residenz where she is supposed to live with the Evergardens normal life. Due to her growing up in the military she has a hard time adjusting to a civil life so Hodgins decides to show her to his business CH postal company There she witness an Automemory doll as she was writing a letter for a illiteralte man who wants to proclaim his love to someone. Violet remembers the words 'I love you' which were said to her by Gilbert and her persue of learning the meaning of those words she decides to become an Automemory doll. Hodgins surprised by her showing signs of her free will and not acting on orders decides to accept her proposal despite her not having any experience in that field of work.

CH postal company

Violet becomes an automemory doll apprentice but due to her background she is too straightforward and struggles to understand the clients needs. So after disccusing with her superior Cattleya and Hodgins deside to put her into 'Auto Memories Doll Training School'.
There Violet befrieds a girl named Luculia Marlborough, Violet always gets top marks from grammar and typing speed despite that she struggles with trying to write letter on behalf of Luculia as she was instructed to do.
Luculia then proceeds to take her to her favourite place which is the top of bell tower there to enjoy the views, she tells her about her brother who was cripped in the war and now is drunkard.
Next day Luculia graduates with top grades but Violet fails due to her inability to write an actual letter that is report like. Luculia wants to help Violet so they meet and she offers Violet to write a letter to Major Gilbert on her behalf, but Violet cant find the right words to say.
Luculia then says how she is unable to convey her feelings towards her brother feels guilty that he failed to protect his parents who died in the war, but she is just glad he is alive and home.
Luculia then leaves but Violet writes a short but effective letter to Luculia's brother on her behalf. She then gives it to Luculia's brother. Luculia touched shows it to the instructor and Violet graduates.

later life

With every new job as an automemory doll Violet gets better and better at understanding and conveying emotions. Later after meeting Gilbert she resigns from the CH postal company to live with him on the island

Appearance

Violet is described as a really beautiful young girl with some childish features given her young age which gives her doll-like appearance.
She is of a slim build with medium height. She has big blue eyes with dark thick eyelashes.
Her long hair ends at her hips but often wears up, she has a fringe with two face-framing pieces. Due to her losing arms in war she has silver prosthetic arms.
She is often seen wearing her work attire which consists of a white dress with a Persian blue jacket with brown gloves
and long-heeled dark brown leather boots. On her ribbon tie, she wears an emerald brooch which was a gift from Gilbert.
