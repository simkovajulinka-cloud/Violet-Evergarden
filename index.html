Violet Evergarden

What it is?

Violet Evergarden is an anime from 2018, based on the light novel series written by Kana Akatsuki. Produced by Kyoto Animation Studio.
It's about a young girl called Violet Evergarden, who was raised to be a child soldier, after she was found as a orphan on the battle field.
After the war ended she becomes a Auto Memory doll in her persue of learning what 'I love you' means.

What it is about

Violet Evergarden is about a young girl, Violet, who was raised as a soldier and must adjust to life after the war.
She starts working as a professional letter writer, called an Auto Memory Doll, helping people express their feelings.
Through this work, she learns about emotions, love, and the meaning of human connection.
